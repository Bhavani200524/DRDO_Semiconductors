# DRDO_Semiconductors
Participation in DRDO-sponsored seminar on semiconductor devices and sensors, focusing on advanced technologies and real-world applications.

##  Overview
Participated in a DRDO-sponsored national seminar on **“Semiconductor Devices and Sensors Breakthroughs”** conducted at SR University.  
The seminar focused on recent advancements in semiconductor technology and sensor applications in defense and modern electronics.

##  Key Learnings
- Fundamentals of semiconductor devices
- Working principles of sensors
- Emerging semiconductor technologies
- Role of sensors in real-world applications
- Applications in defense and military systems


## Topics Covered
- Semiconductor Device Basics (Diodes, Transistors)
- Sensor Technologies (Temperature, Pressure, Gas Sensors)
- Device Fabrication Concepts
- Advanced Semiconductor Materials
- Integration of sensors in embedded systems


## Applications Discussed
- Defense and military systems
- Smart sensing and automation
- IoT-based monitoring systems
- Industrial and environmental sensing


##  Skills Gained
- Understanding of semiconductor fundamentals
- Knowledge of sensor technologies
- Exposure to real-world applications
- Analytical and conceptual learning
